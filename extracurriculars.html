Extracurriculars






Marching Band

I am a member of the New Providence High School Marching Band. As a freshman, I was a member of the Pit. As a sophomore, junior, and senior, I played the Bass Drum. My freshman and junior years, the band received awards for being State and National Champions.






Boy Scouts

I am a Life Scout, and am waiting on my Board of Review to become an Eagle Scout. I have served as both a Senior Patrol Leader (SPL) and Assistant Senior Patrol Leader (ASPL). The SPL is the highest-ranking youth leader in the troop. The ASPL is second-highest rank, and helps the SPL to run the troop and lead troop meetings and activities.
