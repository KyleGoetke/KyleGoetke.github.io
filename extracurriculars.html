Extracurriculars






Marching Band

I am a member of the New Providence High School Marching Band. As a freshman, I was a member of the Pit. As a sophomore, junior, and senior, I played the Bass Drum. My freshman, junior, and senior years, the band received awards for being both State and National Champions.






Boy Scouts

I am an Eagle Scout and an active leader in my troop. I have served as a Senior Patrol Leader, and current serve as a Junior Assistant Scoutmaster. As an SPL, I led and planned troop meetings, campouts, and other activities. As a JASM, I instruct younger scouts in the troop and supported the troop's adult leadership.






Appalachia Service Project

I volunteered in Virginia and West Virgina with the Appalachia Service Project to repair homes and improve living conditions for low-income families. ASP has been helping communities in central Appalachia for the past 50 years, and being a part of it was a challenging but fulfilling experience. My team and I worked closely with the homeowners to give them the best possible results in a limited timeframe. While the work was difficult, it was an incredible, life-changing experience.
