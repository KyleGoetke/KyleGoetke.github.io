Extracurriculars






Marching Band

I am a member of the New Providence High School Marching Band. As a freshman, I was a member of the Pit. As a sophomore, junior, and senior, I played the Bass Drum. My freshman and junior years, the band received awards for being State and National Champions.






Boy Scouts

I am a Life Scout, and am awaiting my Eagle Scout Board of Review. I have served as both a Senior Patrol Leader (SPL) and Assistant Senior Patrol Leader (ASPL). The SPL is the highest-ranking youth leader in the troop and leads the troop meetings, troop campouts, and other troop activities.






Appalachia Service Project

I volunteered in Virginia and West Virgina with the Appalachia Service Project to repair homes and improve living conditions for low-income families. ASP has been helping communities in central Appalachia for the past 50 years, and being a part of it was a challenging but fulfilling experience. My team and I worked closely with the homeowners to give them the best possible results in a limited timeframe. While the work was difficult, it was an incredible, life-changing experience.
