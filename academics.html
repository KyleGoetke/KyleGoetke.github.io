Academics







These are projects from high school that I am proud to show off. I enjoy giving presentations, so several are included to show the growth and development of my presentation skills over the last several years.







Scientific Inquiry and Analysis:
Virtual Reality Presentation (2016)

This project explored the up-and-coming technology of Virtual Reality. My group researched its constraints, impacts, and usefulness. I selected this project because it was my favorite from this class. I learned about the rapidly expanding VR industry and how VR could be useful for education.









Honors Biology:
Muscular Dystrophy Presentation (2017)

For this project, I researched Muscular Dystrophy and its genetic causes. I selected this project because I foudn the topic very interesting. I got to learn about how a single gene can completely change peoples' lives. This project also helped me learn about genetic mutations and how they can impact the human body.

Honors Physics:
Projectile Launcher (2018)

For this project, I had to design a projectile launcher that would launch a projectile at a certain angle. Then, using equations I had learned in class, I calculated other information about the projectile, like its initial velocity. If I had more time to work on the project, I would have improved the design of the launcher, as the base was not very stable.
